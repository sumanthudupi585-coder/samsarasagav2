About Samsara Saga

×

Samsara Saga is a spiritual journey through the cycles of existence. In this text-based RPG adventure, you'll explore the concepts of karma, dharma, and the eternal cycle of rebirth.

Your choices will shape your character's path through the game, affecting your spiritual attributes and determining your ultimate destiny.

Key Features:

  • Character creation based on Vedic astrology (Nakshatras)
  • Meaningful choices that affect your spiritual journey
  • Puzzles that challenge your understanding of spiritual concepts
  • Multiple endings based on your karma and spiritual development
